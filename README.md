Project Overview

This project analyzes a healthcare dataset to identify business trends, patient demographics, treatment costs, and insurance coverage. The analysis was performed as part of the NeoAI Technologies Data Analytics Selection Assessment.

Tools Used

Tableau
Microsoft Excel

Business Analysis Performed

* Identified the disease contributing the highest total treatment cost.
* Determined the department generating the highest revenue.
* Calculated the average treatment cost by disease and insurance status.
* Computed the percentage of total treatment costs covered by insured patients.
* Identified the city with the highest average treatment cost per patient.

Patient Demographics Analysis

* Analyzed healthcare expenditure across different age groups (18–30, 31–45, 46–60, 60+).
* Identified the most common diseases among different age groups.
* Compared treatment costs between male and female patients.
* Determined the blood group with the highest incidence of critical outcomes.
